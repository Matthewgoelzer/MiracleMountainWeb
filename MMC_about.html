What We Believe

Jesus Christ, our Salvation

God gave His only son Jesus to save us from our sin (John 3:16).

The Holy Scriptures

The Bible is God-inspired, infallible, and authoritative (2 Timothy 3:16, 17).

Baptism in the Holy Spirit

The Baptism in the Holy Spirit is available to all believers and occurs as described in the Bible
(Act 2:4).

Divine Healing

Physical healing is available to us today through the power of Jesus Christ in answer to the prayer of faith (James 5:14-16).

The Second Coming of Christ

The second coming of Christ is personal and imminent (1 Thessalonians 4:16,17).
