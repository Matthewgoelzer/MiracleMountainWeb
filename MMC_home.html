Camp Speakers

Julianna and Matthew Goelzer

Julianna and Matthew Goelzer are graduate students in science and engineering at Boise State. While they love research and discovery, their true desire is to help others have a relationship that enables them to walk with Him in power and experience his presence. They are excited to share their passion for science and the Creator with the kids at Miracle Mountain.
